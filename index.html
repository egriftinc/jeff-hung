Hello! My Name is Jeff Hung

I'm a 30 year old web developer based in Vancouver, specializing in Web Development and Web Design. I build clean, appealing, and functional websites that also comply with the lastest web standards.

I was born and raised in the city of New York in late 1980's. I had started coding and developing websites on my own since December of 2012. After learning everything by myself and developing over 100 websites over the course of 5 years, I moved to Vancouver and decided to go to BCIT to brush up on the latest of web standards.

I graduated from BCIT as fully certified Web Developer/Designer and have been working as a freelancer ever since. If you have any websites you wish to develop please leave a message below.
